Mega Tall

The World's largest tallest structure is the Burj Khalifa. It is located in Dubai, The United Arab Emirates. It stands an amazing 829.8 meteres (or,2772 feet)tall. Given its remarkable height, the Burj khalifa has been placed into a new category of skyscraper called a "MEGATALL SKYSCRAPER". The building also has 163 floors. To reach these floor, it has 57 elevators and 8 escalators. It has over 24000 windows. The windows at the very top of the building are washed by robots.
